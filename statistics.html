About India!

There are

197

Hydropower plants in India.

Ranks

#5

Globally for installed hydroelectric power capacity.

But Ranks

#4

Globally for underdeveloped hydropower potential.

India can produce

12.3%

of its total utility power generation capacity through hydropower.

The International Hydropower Association estimates that the total hydropower potential in India is

660,000 GWh/year

of which 540,000 GWh/year (79%) is still undeveloped.
